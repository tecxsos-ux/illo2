From a Hong Kong factory floor to your everyday.

EASYMATE has been cutting, sewing and checking colourful organisers since 1986. ilo is our line for Europe.

1986: where it started

EASYMATE began as a small Hong Kong workshop making pencil cases and document pouches for local stationers. Four decades later we run full sewing lines — but every piece still passes through the same hands-on quality checks we started with.

Because we own the factory, we control everything: materials, colours, stitching and price. The ilo line is our way of bringing that factory-direct honesty to Switzerland and Europe.

“If a zip doesn't glide, it doesn't ship. That has been the rule since day one.”

— Factory QC lead, EASYMATE Hong Kong

Materials we trust

EVA

Soft, crystal-clear and water-resistant. Our clear pouches wipe clean in seconds and stay flexible in the cold.

Mesh

Airy, washable and see-through at a glance. Double-stitched seams keep featherweight pouches surprisingly tough.

Nylon

Colourfast, abrasion-resistant and light. The backbone of our crossbody bags, sleeves and travel organisers.
